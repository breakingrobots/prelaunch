About SiteClimate

SiteClimate was formed to address the incident and alert management woes that plague the technology industry. We set out to make a simple and clean interface for understanding what's going on in our production environment and, in turn, learned how to make incident management something that's zenful and intuitive. We do it by employing cutting edge machine learning technology to make intelligent decisions about what's important and what's noise and help you and your operations teams focus on where the root cause of the problem actually is.
